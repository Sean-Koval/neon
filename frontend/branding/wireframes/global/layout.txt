================================================================================
ROOT LAYOUT — layout.tsx
================================================================================
The shell that wraps every page: fixed sidebar + scrollable main area.
Global overlays (command palette, status bar) are mounted here.

VIEWPORT: Full browser window (min 1280px designed, responsive down to 1024px)

COMPONENT HIERARCHY:
  RootLayout
  ├── Providers
  │   ├── ThemeProvider (next-themes)
  │   ├── ToastProvider
  │   ├── AuthProvider
  │   ├── tRPC.Provider
  │   └── QueryClientProvider
  ├── Sidebar (always visible, always dark)
  ├── <main> (scrollable content area)
  │   └── {children} (page component)
  ├── CommandPalette (mounted but hidden until Cmd+K)
  ├── StatusBar (mounted but hidden until work is running)
  └── KeyboardShortcutsOverlay (mounted but hidden until ?)

RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR:
  >= 1280px: Full sidebar (w-64) + content
  1024-1279px: Collapsed sidebar (w-16, icons only) + content
  < 1024px: Hidden sidebar + hamburger menu (future consideration)
